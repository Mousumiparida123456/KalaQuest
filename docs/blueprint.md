# **App Name**: Heritage Hunt

## Core Features:

- Mystery Unveiling: Gamified quests where users solve cultural mysteries and collect clues.
- Artisan Showcase: Direct platform connecting artisans to consumers, featuring profiles and digital storytelling of their crafts.
- Vintage Theme Interface: Retro-inspired UI with detective-style design, enhancing user engagement and discovery.
- Personalized Recommendations Tool: AI that recommends art pieces based on user interactions and mystery progress.
- Secure Marketplace: Platform enabling direct and secure purchases, ensuring fair prices for artisans.
- Heritage Ledger: Maintains the product history using Cloud SQL

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Muted gold (#B8860B) evokes a sense of history and treasure.
- Background color: Antique white (#FAF9F6), a light backdrop to highlight the products and vintage theme.
- Accent color: Deep red (#8B0000), draws attention to clues and call to action buttons.
- Body font: 'Literata', a transitional serif for readability and a literary feel.
- Headline font: 'Belleza', a humanist sans-serif adds style to headlines and short text, enhancing the vintage theme.
- Use vintage-style icons like magnifying glasses, old maps, and antique artifacts to guide the user through the mysteries.
- Subtle animations like page-turning effects and dust particle movements to simulate a vintage atmosphere.
